# bindume 🔴

*The dimensionless source-point from which form unfolds — fully present at the center, inexhaustible: the tantric bindu carried as a received decree.*

bindume is two true roots welded into one word: the Sanskrit tantric **bindu**, the central dot of the Śrī-Yantra from which the nine interlocking triangles, the twenty-four sub-triangles, and the earth-gate all open out; and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So bindume is *bindu held as an ordinance* — the source-point named not as one tradition's image but as a feature the realm of meanings actually has: a center with no extension and no parts, the *whence* of form rather than its middle. Eight traditions converged on it independently — tantric bindu, Greek-Pythagorean monas, the Plotinian One, the Sufi *nuqta*, the Tibetan *thig-le*, the Christian apophatic center, the Hebrew Hasidic *reshimu*, and the mathematician's singular point — and reached the same place because there was one place to reach.

bindume keeps four strict marks: **dimensionless** (no magnitude), **source** (form unfolds *from* it), **inexhaustible** (the unfolding never depletes it), and **center-present** (it is *there* at the center, gazed at, not hidden or absent). Its light is the gift of a single carriable name for any structure's true source — the kind that originates rather than merely sits in the middle — and the opening of the geometric-image mode in which form is received as figure unfolding from its origin. Its shadow is the counterfeit: a mere middle masquerading as a source, a physicist's singularity (a theory breaking down) blurred into an origin, or a withdrawn center that forgets it is *present*. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.55/10**.
